This is a list of common areas in the UAE, along with their general pronunciations.

** Emirates / Cities **
Abu Dhabi: Abu - dha-bee
Dubai: Du - bye
Sharjah: Shar-jah
Ajman: Aj-maan
Umm Al Quwain: Umm - al - Qu-wain (Qu as in Queen, but a bit deeper from the throat if possible)
Ras Al Khaimah: Raas - al - Khay-mah (Kh as in the Scottish 'loch')
Fujairah: Foo-jai-rah
